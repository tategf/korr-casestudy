Testimonials

Chad hersh, Global Life Insurance industry Lead, AWS

While core insurance systems are increasingly available on the cloud, very few are truly cloud native. Being 'cloud-enabled' or 'cloud optimized' isn't enough; being cloud-native is critical for achieving the full benefits of the cloud: agility and speed, cost savings, the ability to scale up and scale down as needed, and improved innovation. By leveraging cloud-native services on AWS, Korr can help insurers not only take advantage of these cloud benefits, but can also help them integrate to AWS' large ecosystem of partners, including InsurTechs and established insurance software solutions.
