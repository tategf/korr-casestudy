Product

No more wrestling with legacy apps or spending a fortune on custom solutions. With Korr, you get back to what you do best: matching your customers with the perfect plans to put their minds at ease.

  1. Concept

    Insurance is all about expecting the unexpected. Agents need a platform that lets them rapidly adapt to unforeseen changes. Speed is the guiding philosophy driving Korr.

  2. Structure

    As a cloud-native web solution, Korr updates seamlessly and is incredibly simple to maintain. Begin with an existing line-of-business configuration or easily develop an entirely new one, Korr's system configuration is data-driven enabling rapid improvements to your business. IT is no longer the governor of the business's ambitions and abilities.

  3. Build

    Korr leverages AWS, the most reliable infrastructure for cloud-native software. Our schema-less data model ensures that Korr captures each insurer's unique requirements, and enables rapid and complete migration of historic data from legacy systems — Korr does the hard work of maintaining business continuity while enabling you to fully decommission expensive and obsolete systems.

  4. Product

    Korr's interface is intuitive, friendly and productive right out of the box. Our system-wide Google-like search enables users to quickly find the information they require — our app works for them, not the other way around.

Testimonials

Chad hersh, Global Life Insurance industry Lead, AWS

While core insurance systems are increasingly available on the cloud, very few are truly cloud native. Being 'cloud-enabled' or 'cloud optimized' isn't enough; being cloud-native is critical for achieving the full benefits of the cloud: agility and speed, cost savings, the ability to scale up and scale down as needed, and improved innovation. By leveraging cloud-native services on AWS, Korr can help insurers not only take advantage of these cloud benefits, but can also help them integrate to AWS' large ecosystem of partners, including InsurTechs and established insurance software solutions.
